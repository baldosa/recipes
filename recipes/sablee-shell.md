ingredients:
  - qty: 250g
    name: Cake flour
  - qty: 150g
    name: chill butter
  - qty: 150g
    name: sugar
  - qty: 1
    name: eggs
instructions:
  - In a stand mixer, mix the flour, butter and sugar.
  - Add the egg and mix.
  - Put the dough in the counter and mix with the hands without kneading. 
  - Put the dough in a ziploc bag and refigerate for 15 min
  - Using a rolling pin, roll out the dough to a thickness of 5 cm and place it in a 24cm pan. With a fork, prick it.
  - Put it in the pan on the freezer for 15 minutes. 
  - Cook in a preheated oven at 180C for 15 minutes
  - Let it cool down in the pan.
