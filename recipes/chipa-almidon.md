---
layout: recipe.njk
title: Chipa de almidón
description: Sandwich bread
prep_time: 15 min
cooking_time: 30 min
servings: X
tip: make lots and freeze
difficulty: easy
tags:
  - breakfast
  - snack
ingredients:
  - qty: 500g
    name: yuca starch
  - qty: 150g
    name: butter
  - qty: 2
    name: eggs
  - qty: 100g
    name: milk
  - qty: 5g
    name: salt
  - qty: 350g
    name: cheese

instructions:
  - Mix starch with salt
  - Add cubed butter and mix until sand-like texture
  - Add cubed cheese and mix
  - In a cup, mix egg and milk then add it to the drys
  - Form 3cm diameter balls
  - Oven at 200C+ (high) for 30 min
