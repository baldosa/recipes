ingredients:
  - qty: 1
    name: egg
  - qty: 4g
    name: instant yeast
  - qty: 60g
    name: honey
  - qty: 165g
    name: warm milk
  - qty: 60g
    name: warm water
  - qty: 9g
    name: salt
  - qty: 410g
    name: bread flour

instructions:
  - Add your yeast and mix together all wet ingredients in a bowl. Whisk the egg before combining.
  - Add the bread flour gradually and mix until a soft dough forms.
  - Rest the dough (covered) for about 15 minutes.
  - Then continue kneading until the gluten has started to develop and the dough comes together.
  - Add salt and knead for 2-3 more minutes till the dough passes a light windowpane test.
  - Cover and let rise until doubled, about 1 hour.
  - Gently shape into a loaf.
  - Place into a buttered ~24cm by ~10cm loaf pan. Cover and let rise until puffy but still slightly springy when pressed, about 20-30 minutes.
  - Preheat oven to 180C (mid).
  - Brush lightly over the loaf with egg wash.
  - Bake for 30-35 minutes.
  - Cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then remove and cool completely before slicing.
